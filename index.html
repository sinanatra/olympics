DATA STORYTELLING - 100 YEARS OF OLYMPIC HISTORY is an installation visualizing all 22,560 athletes who have ever participated in the Olympic Winter Games since their foundation in 1924.

It is currently on display at Le Gallerie di Piedicastello in Trento as part of PERFORMANCE: a preparatory exhibition for the Milan Cortina 2026 edition of the Winter Games.

Here you can explore both curated tales and stories that may be of personal interest.
